 Yoyo Market - Winter Holiday Details  
  (Friday, December 28th, 2012)

 

Yoyo Market will be closed for the winter holidays from January 1st until January 6th, 2013. All orders placed after 12 noon on December 31st will be shipped on January 7th.

 

We hope everyone has a wonderful holiday, and we look forward to the opportunity to serve you in the new year!

 

 

 Yoyo Market Now Carries 'Marley Coffee'!  
  (Friday, August 28th, 2012)

 

Yoyo Market is proud to announce that it now carries Marley Coffee! We currently have three flavors in stock: Lively Up!, One Love, and Simmer Down (DECAF!!!)

 

Marley Coffee is an international gourmet coffee brand founded by reggae legend Bob Marley's son, Rohan. As a brand, "Marley Coffee is determined to deliver that same intoxicating aroma and rich smooth flavor into every cup."

 'Yoyo Rewards' Launched!  
  (Friday, August 10th, 2012)

 

Do you have an account on YoyoMarket.jp? If not, you should sign up! Not only is it free to become a member, but beginning today you will receive points for every order you make. Those points can be turned into cash and used as a discount on any future order! For every 100 yen you spend, you get one point. When you want to use your points, 1 point = 1 yen!

 

For more information, check out the Yoyo Rewards page here.
