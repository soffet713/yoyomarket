Answer from Jason
Hey everyone! If you are on this page, you may be asking yourself, "what is the Yoyo Market Newsletter?" Well, let me break it down for you...we here at Yoyo Market like to communicate with our customers and let everyone know what's going on. Every other week we publish the Yoyo Market Newsletter to update everyone on what's new with us and provide helpful information, recipes, etc. regarding our great products to help brighten or spice up your week. Scroll down to see our archive of the current newsletter as well as past ones, just in case they accidentally got deleted from your inbox and you really want to find that one Yoyo Market item or a great recipe we recommended to impress your friends and family!
