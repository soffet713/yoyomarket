Product Pages

To get the lowdown on a product, just click on it and it will take you to the product page. Here you will see a brief description along with the product weight written below the green add to cart button.

 

Product Pages

 

Adding an item to your cart is easy - just click on the green add to cart button.

 

Not sure how much we charge for shipping? Each box costs 950 yen for up to 25 kilograms. The weight is listed below the add to cart button, so just add the total of your product weights and as long as you are under 25 kilograms, you will only be charged for one box. Yoyo Market does not charge extra for shipping frozen or chilled items, so don't worry, you can add whatever you want to your shopping cart and we'll handle the rest.

 

For orders over 30,000 yen, shipping is free. So, for those of you who don't want to pay for shipping or worry about keeping track of the weights of your items, this is your option!

The Shopping Cart

Once you are ready to check out, first click on the shopping cart icon at the very top of the page. This will show you what is in your shopping cart. Here you can edit the amount of each item in the cart, then press the 'update cart' button to change the item total in your cart as well as the total cost of your order. You can also click 'continue Shopping' or even 'empty cart', however if you'd like to proceed with your purchase, simply press 'checkout'.

The Checkout Page

Once you've clicked 'checkout' you'll be taken to the checkout page where you'll again be shown the items in your cart. Below the list of your items, there are blank fields to fill in for your bill to and ship to addresses.

Bill to Address Section

 

For the first blank, if we are shipping to your company, please include your company name. If we are not shipping to your company building or office, there is no need to include your company name.

 

Next, continue filling in the remaining blanks as instructed.

 

For those who want to enter a foreign billing address, you can, but unfortunately we cannot change the drop down menu to include countries other than Japan. Please ignore this dropdown menu, and write your country name in the city/ward field.

 

If your ship to address is the same as your bill to address, check the 'same as the Ship to Address' box at the bottom of the 'Bill To Address' section and save yourself some time!


Ship to Address Section

 

Please fill in the blanks as instructed...

 

IMPORTANT - As we shop at Costco, there are occasionally times where a product will be out of stock. In that case, we will often try giving you a call and suggesting another product, or ask if you'd like to make any changes to your order. Therefore, it is IMPORTANT that you give us a phone number that we will be able contact you at.

 

Have any special requests? If you have anything you'd like us to do or pay special attention to, you can use the Order Comments/Request field to let us know.

Shipping Options

This is the section that will show you how much your shipping will cost. Simply click the 'Calculate Shipping' button to see the shipping options dropdown menu. For those who have over 30,000 yen in your shopping cart, make sure to choose FREE SHIPPING in this section.

 

When will your package arrive? If you order before 12 noon from Monday to Thursday, we can ship your order the same day. On Fridays, you have until 13:00 to place your order and get same day shipping. Orders placed after 13:00 on Friday will be shipped the following Monday.

 

Please be aware that for orders shipping to SHIKOKU, YAMAGUCHI, SHIMANE, HIROSHIMA, OKAYAMA, TOTTORI, SADO ISLAND, WAKAYAMA, AKITA, and AOMORI prefectures, next day delivery is NOT AVAILABLE BEFORE 14:00.

 

For customers in OKINAWA, KYUSHU, and HOKKAIDO your order will take two days to ship. The same 12 noon on weekdays, and 13:00 on Friday order deadline applies. 

 

SHOPPING HINT! - If you have missed the daily shipping deadline for same day shipping, try choosing 'no preference' as your delivery date in the Payment Info section. If we are still at the store and we receive your order with a 'no preference' delivery date, we'll do our best to buy all of your items and get them shipped the same day. We can't promise that we'll be able to every time, but if we can, we will. We aim to please! .

Payment Info

OPTION 1 - Cash on Delivery (COD) - You can choose to pay cash to the delivery driver at the time of delivery. There is a 350 yen surchase for this option.

 

OPTION 2 - Credit Card (NOTE: You do NOT need a Paypal account to pay with your credit card) - You can also choose to pay by credit card. To do so, select 'Credit Card' as your payment type, and once all other required fields are filled out, click the 'submit my order' button at the bottom of the page. Doing so will take you to our Paypal payment processing page.

 

Here you will have two choices. If you are a Paypal member, you can log in and pay with your Paypal account. If you are not a Paypal member, click on the link at the bottom that says "Don't Have a Paypal Account?". This will take you to a new page where you can input your credit card information as you normally would and pay with your debit or credit card as a "Paypal Guest."

 

PAYMENT TIP!!! Having problems getting your payment to go through? Paypal confirms all credit card payments on our site and occasionally does not seem to like it when the ship to and bill to addresses are different. If you are trying to use a foreign based credit card, please us your foreign address in both the ship to and bill to fields. Don't worry! We already have your Japanese shipping address from our website where you placed the order.

 

* After you have completed your order you will receive an automatic confirmation email from us outlining the details of your order

 

* After we have shipped your order you will receive another automatic email notifying you that your order has been shipped
